Overview

Blue Moon was founded in 2011 at Montgomery High School in Skillman, New Jersey. Blue Moon offers quality, low-cost music lessons in the surrounding community.

Our tutors are some of Montgomery's finest young musicians. Many particapte in MHS's music ensembles and have also participated in regional and state ensembles.

Most importantly, they have all been through the music program, and have much knowledge and experience to share with students!

Donations

Blue Moon is a non-profit organization and has donated over $16,000. Donations are targeted towards improving music education in underfunded communities
